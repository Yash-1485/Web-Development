Sanand Branch Hospital
  • West End Health Campus is a sprawling, multi-building medical facility that offers a wide range of specialized healthcare services, including both inpatient and outpatient care.
  • The branch is known for its advanced cardiac center, which offers treatments for heart diseases, including interventional cardiology and bypass surgeries.
  • It also features a dedicated cancer care unit with cutting-edge treatments such as immunotherapy and radiation therapy.
  • The hospital offers rehabilitation services to aid in recovery from surgeries and chronic conditions.
  • With a focus on providing comprehensive care, West End Health Campus is committed to delivering personalized treatment plans for all patients.
